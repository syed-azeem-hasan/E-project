Jumping Frog

The frog can be a popular animal in origami due to its symbolic meaning. A common association with frogs is the wise spending of money and the receiving of money in return. This is one reason origami frogs can be so popular to make from a napkin at the dinner table, just when the check is about to come.


Step of Instructions

1. Take a square piece of paper and fold in half.

2. Fold one of the corners to touch the other side.

3. Unfold and repeat on the opposite corner.

4. Fold the sides of the rectangular part to the center.

5. Fold the bottom rectangle to the point.

6. Take the corners that are touching and pull them out.

7. Fold the sides of the rectangular part to the center.

8. Fold the bottom rectangle to the point.

9. Take the corners that are touching and pull them out.

10. Fold down the legs.

11. Fold over each legs

12. Fold the frog in half.

13. Fold the bottom part that.

14. Flip it over and there's your jumping frog!
