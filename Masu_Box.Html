Masu Box

The Masu Box, translated as measurement box is a simple origami structure made of paper. This square box can be used to wrap gifts or store objects, but it can also be adapted with simple bookbinding techniques to make sculptural book objects.


Step of Instructions

1. This is the back of the paper, usually white. This side will not be visible on the final box.

2. This is the front of the paper, your box will end up being this colour. Start this side up.

3. Fold the bottom edge up to the top edge.

4. Unfold the previous step.

5. Fold the right edge over to the left edge.

6. Unfold the previous step.

7. Flip the paper over to the other side.

8. Rotate the paper.

9. Fold the bottom corner up to meet the very center, where the two diagonal lines intersect.

10. Repeat the last step on the other three corners.

11. Next, fold the bottom edge up to the middle.

12. Fold the top edge down to meet the bottom section.

13. Unfold the previous two steps.

14. Fold the left and right edges to the middle.

15. Unfold the left and right edges.

16. Unfold the left and right sections completely.

17. Fold the top and bottom edges in to the middle, using the creases that already exist.

18. Open up the middle section, fold the left and right section upwards as well.

19. Bring the right section up and over to the middle.

20. Repeat the same on the left section.

21. The origami masu box is complete.

22. To create a lid for your masu box, we will do the same process with a small difference. Start with your paper colour side up, make the same beginning creases.

23. Flip the paper over to the other side.

24. Fold one corner in the center.

25. Fold the other three corners to the center.

26. Fold the bottom edge up, this time do not fold all the way up to the middle, leave a small gap of 1-2 mm.

27. Fold the top edge down, leaving the same 1-2mm gap.

28. Unfold the previous two steps.

29. Fold the left and right edges to the middle, leaving the same 1-2mm gap from the middle.

30. Unfold the previous step.

31. Open out the left and right flaps.

32. Re-fold the top and bottom edges.

33. Open out the middle box section and bring the left and right sections upwards.

34. Close the right section of the masu box.

35. Complete the box by folding the left section.

36. Now you can use the second box as a lid.
